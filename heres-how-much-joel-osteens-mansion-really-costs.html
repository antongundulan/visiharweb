Here's How Much Joel Osteen's Mansion Really Costs

Joel Osteen lives in his native Houston, Texas. It's his home city, after all, and the city where he makes his grand living. In 2010, Osteen and his wife, Victoria, bought a mansion in the River Oaks suburb of the city. They live there with their two children: daughter Alexandra and son Jonathan. For the last decade, the Osteen family has been living there. The house is located in the wealthy subdivision of Tall Timbers.

Their home is 17,000 square feet and sits on close to two acres of land (via CultureMap Houston). The Osteen house, which boasts five wood fireplaces, has a total of six bedrooms, six bathrooms, three elevators, a guest house, a large outdoor pool in the backyard, and a pool house. Quite a lot of space for a family of four. Nevertheless, it didn't come cheap. Osteen paid $10.5 million for it in 2010, noted Velvet Ropes. It is very likely the value of the home is higher now. Celebrity Net Worth reports that Joel Osteen's house may be worth between $14 to $16 million today.

The couple reportedly also has a 5,600-square-foot home in the Tanglewood area of Houston. That house is worth about $2.9 million, according to Distractify.
