Matt Damon's wife renews eight years of marriage in cream Naeem Kham gown

    After eight years of marriage, Matt Damon and wife Luciana Barrosso renewed their wedding vows in a romantic ceremony on the Caribbean island of St. Lucia.Walking up the aisle in a flowing chiffon dress hot off the catwalk, Matt's wife Luciana said "I do" to her husband for the second time in a cream Naeem Khan design.

    The floor-length dress, which was first seen on the catwalk in the Autumn/Winter 2013 collection of Naeem Khan this February, features a large Grecian style belt to cinch in the waist and create a flattering silhouette. The style of the a deep, plunging wrap cut is a classic amongst the designer's elegant creations and was the perfect choice to show off the Argentinean-born beauty's feminine figure. ' star Vanessa Hudgens and George Clooney's partner Stacy Keibler have also been spotted wearing Naeem Khan's designs this year and the designer has proved a popular choice among the whole of the Damon family. The couple's daughters were also said to be sporting Naeem dresses at the event whilst the groom dressed down in beach appropriate attire with a smart yet casual tan-coloured suit and flip-flops. The four young girls Isabella, six, Gia, four, Stella, two, and Alexia — Luciana's daughter from a precious marriage — were among 50 of the family and friends invited to the Caribbean coast to witness the couple's union.

    Ben Affleck and his wife Jennifer Garner were among many of the famous faces at the star-studded affair which also reportedly included appearances from Chris Hemsworth and British actress Emily Blunt. Matt and Luciana Damon met in a Miami bar in 2003. She was working as a bartender at the bar, while he was shooting his comedy flick . Two years later, the lovebirds became husband and wife with a winter wedding in New York.
