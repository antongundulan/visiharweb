Jewel-Toned Fashion Will Be Everywhere, and You Can Get It at Amazon

When I switched my wardrobe over for winter, I realized that most of my sweaters and dresses are black, gray, or beige. And while there’s nothing wrong with stocking up on neutral basics, it could use a bit of color, too. The Drop Vivienne Padded-Shoulder Balloon-Sleeve Sweater

An easy way to start incorporating color into your winter wardrobe is with a bold sweater like this balloon-sleeve style from The Drop. Available in a vivid purple shade, the top has subtle padded shoulders, exaggerated cuffs, and a ribbed band around the hemline. Wear it casually with jeans and loafers or dress it up with a silky skirt and heeled boots. And the best part is that it’s on sale for 61 percent off, coming out to just $17.

Merokeety V-Neck Cable Knit Sweater Dress With Belt

Another knit style, this Merokeety sweater dress comes in 26 colors, including navy blue, emerald green, and deep burgundy. Along with a cable-knit design, it has a V-neckline, flattering fabric tie around the waist, and a side slit. One reviewer was “shocked at how soft it was,” since it “feels almost like cashmere” for less than $30. That same person went on to say it “hugs all the curves and has good stretch.”

C.Paravano Slingback Pointed-Toe Slingback Flats

If you’re not quite ready to wear colorful clothing, start with a pair of shoes instead. These C.Paravano slingback flats come in a beautiful deep red that would look great with everything from jeans and a tee to a formal dress. They’re made of patent leather with a chic pointed toe, two straps, and padded insoles. A reviewer confirmed the shoes are “surprisingly comfortable” and “very cute to dress up or down.” 

The Drop Addison Soft Volume Top-Handle Bag

A bright bag is another easy way to add a pop of color, and this red top-handle purse from The Drop will pair nicely with your new flats. Made from soft faux leather, it has a ruched handle, lined, roomy interior, and a magnetic snap closure. According to a reviewer, it’s “super lightweight” and “big enough to fit your keys, phone, small wallet, and a few other small items,” making it the perfect finishing touch for a night out. 

Pavoi 14-Karat Gold-Plated Sterling Silver Post Huggies

For a super subtle (and literal) ode to the jewel tones trend, check out these Pavoi 14-karat gold-plated huggies, which are available with green, red, navy, or multi-colored cubic zirconia stones, as well as a versatile black option. They have sterling silver posts to prevent irritation and secure clasps to keep them in place. And at just $12, you “can’t beat the price for earrings that don’t tarnish,” as one shopper put it.
