Who is Glow Up’s Leigh?

Leigh is a finalist on the BBC series Glow Up: Britain’s Next Make-Up Star.

Outside of the show, Leigh works as a freelance make-up artist.

The 29-year-old currently lives in Manchester, and also works as a painter.
