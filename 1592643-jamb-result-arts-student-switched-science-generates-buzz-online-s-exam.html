JAMB Result of Arts Student Who Switched to Science Generates Buzz Online, Says It's His First Exam

  • The 2024 Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) score of a young lad who changed his department to science has stirred reactions online
  • The formerly arts student stated that the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) exam was his first as a science student
  • While reacting to his performance in the exam, the boy revealed what made him switch from arts to science

A science student, Paul Daniel Chukwudi, has expressed gratitude to God for his UTME performance.

Paul took to Facebook to display his UTME score, revealing that he switched from arts department to science.

Paul noted that he made the switch due to the course he was going for, adding that the UTME was his first exam as a science student.

Paul had 195 on aggregate. The former arts student scored 63 in English, 42 in mathematics, 45 in physics and 45 in biology. Paul wrote:

Science student scores low in UTME

In another related news, a science student had performed poorly in the UTME.

After checking the results online, the student took to Facebook to share the scores for people to see. The JAMB UTME scores shared on Facebook showed that the student scored a total of 162 marks. The subjects he took were English language, mathematics, biology, chemistry and physics.

Science student's UTME score trends

Meanwhile, Legit.ng previously reported that a science student had scored 17 in physics in the 2024 UTME.
