The Rocks Daughter Ava Kidnapped During WWE NXT Heatwave

Update: Ava was freed after the conditions for her release were met.

Original: The Creed Brothers lost a loser leaves NXT match several weeks ago, but they haven’t gone far. They were back, lurking in the shadows where the Schism least expected them. This week during WWE NXT Heatwave, Ava paid the price.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE NXT this week.

The Rock’s daughter turned into a Superstar to walk in her father’s big footsteps. This was in spite of a ton of adversities, including a recovery from a knee injury. This week on NXT, things took a turn for the worst.

After losing her match, Ava sent Ivy Nile a few angry messages, with two members of the Dyad behind her. Those masked men did not speak, but when Ivy Nile showed up later on, they revealed themselves to be the Creed Brothers.

The Creed Brothers and Ivy Nile then locked Ava away and said that if the Schism wants to see her again, they want a cage match next week against the Dyad. If the Creed Brothers win the match, then the are back in NXT.

Ava is already playing this up on X. She posted, “LET ME OUT OF THIS ROOM WTF IVY,” after the segment. We’ll have to see if she gets out.
