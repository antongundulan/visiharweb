Loren Gray Net Worth 2023: What Is The Influencer Worth?

As of 2023, the net worth of Loren Gray, the American singer and internet sensation, is estimated to be around $5 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. This figure is a testament to her successful career as an influencer, singer, and social media personality.

Born in Pottstown, Pennsylvania in April 2002, Loren Gray rose to fame through her engaging content on TikTok. She joined the platform when it was still known as musical.ly in 2015. From there, she quickly gained a large following while still in the 6th grade. At one point, she held the title of the most-followed account on TikTok, a testament to her popularity and influence.
