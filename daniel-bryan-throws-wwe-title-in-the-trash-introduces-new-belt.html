Daniel Bryan Throws WWE Title In The Trash &amp; Introduces New Belt

Daniel Bryan is a man with a lot of thoughts and he didn’t like the title he was carrying around at all. It was made from leather and he called the cow that died for his title Daisy.

During SmackDown Live, he introduced Rowan who came out with a bag over his shoulder. Daniel cut a promo about the WWE Title and the symbol it represented.

Suddenly, Erick Rowan grabbed a trash can and then Daniel Bryan threw away the WWE Title and pulled out his own version of the championship.

It is a wooden title made of hemp and carved out of oak. It looks as close to being a WWE Title made out of wood and hemp as you can get. It is certainly a very interesting decision and there were some serious mixed-reactions.
