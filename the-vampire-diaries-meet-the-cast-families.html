Ian Somerhalder

Ian Somerhalder took on the role of Damon, Stefan's much more sarcastic and cynical brother. In 2014, the 42-year-old actor began dating Twilight star Nikki Reed, 32, and the pair tied the knot a year later. The couple welcomed their first child together, a girl named Bodhi Soleil, in 2017.
