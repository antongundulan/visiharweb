Chicago Fire star Kara Killmer shares very rare picture of husband as she reunites with co stars ami

NBC's One Chicago franchise is expected to return in 2024 after the SAG strikes end

    One Chicago may not be returning to screens any time soon but the show's stars are still spending time together.

    Kara Killmer, who plays paramedic Sylvie Brett on took to Instagram to share that she had spent the past evening with costar Joe Minoso, who plays Firefighter Joe Cruz, as well aChicago PD's Marina Squerciati, also known as police officer Kim Burgess, and Chicago Med's Torrey DeVitto and Nick Gehlfuss who both left the series as Doctors Natalie Manning and Will Halstead respectively. 

    But she was also joined by her husband Andrew Cheney, and it was those snaps that had fans over the moon at the rare public outing for the pair. 

    "My hubby, @andrewcheney, and I had an enlightening evening at @msichicago to see #theblueparadox exhibit. It was a delightful night discussing the impact of our use of plastics and it’s impact on our oceans and our health," Kara captioned the post.

    Joe is married to makeup artist Caitlin Murphy Miles after meeting on the set of back in 2013, while mom-of-one Marina has been married to attorney Eli Kay-Oliphant since 2016. 

    Torrey was married to Paul Wesley between 2011 and 2013 but they later divorced. 

    In September 2023 her partner Jared LaPine proposed after six months of dating. 

    The special moment took place in Michigan, and Jared later shared details of the moment, writing on social media: "On September 1st, I got engaged to my best friend.
