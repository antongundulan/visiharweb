The Best Activities for UK Stag Parties

There are few experiences quite like a stag party, serving as a rite of passage for any man planning to get married. It’s one of the last chances a groom will have to truly let go and have fun with his closest friends before he gets married, so it’s understandable why so many decide to go all out. There are countless different things that the group can get up to, and while there are many for you to choose from, we’ve selected just a few that you’ll find most interesting.

1. Visit a casino

Casinos are a popular choice for stag parties, and it’s easy to see why. They’re a place unlike anywhere else, being the perfect place to get loose, have fun, and celebrate a night with friends. You can be sure that the drinks will flow and the atmosphere will be lovely, packed with like-minded patrons looking for a good time. There are countless real money slot games that you can play there, spanning various genres and themes, as well as many other games, such as roulette and blackjack, plus free spins for new players. 

2. Karting

If you fancy being an amateur F1 driver, karting could be the perfect activity for you and your friends. While many of the options we’ve included here will include alcohol, such as the culture in the UK, this one will have to be done sober, though that won’t make it any less fun. Luckily, there are racing tracks all over the county, and we’re sure you will be close to one, which is ideal if you want to go out and continue the party afterward. 

3. Paintball

Like karting, paintball is a great way to have fun without drinking, and while nothing is stopping you from drinking afterward, this is an activity you’ll want to do without any intoxication. This game might be fun, but it also takes serious levels of skill, and you can be sure that at least one person in your stag party will take it too seriously and overdo it. 

4. Pub Crawl

A staple of any British stag party, you can expect a pub crawl to feature when it’s your turn to get married. As one of the most reliable activities that will go down well with the rest of your party, the drinks will flow, and we’re sure it’ll run well into the night. It’s a chance to experience drinking in a new city, adding to the mystery and increasing the likelihood of you spending the last part of the night eating kebabs or piling into a McDonald’s.

5. Zorb football

A more modern addition to the plethora of options available to the stag party, zorb football has taken the UK (as well as social media) by storm in recent years, and it’s easy to see why. If you like being outside your comfort zone and are okay with a couple of bumps and bruises, this is a great activity that we’re sure you and your stag party will love.
