Classic Pork Roll, Egg &amp; Cheese Sandwich | Recipe

Rachael considers herself "the Queen of the Burger," but calls Johnny Yarusi "the King of the Breakfast Sandwich." The pork roll sandwich is iconic in New Jersey, and Rach says Johnny makes the best! He layers a hard roll with seared pork roll slices, American cheese, a fried egg, ketchup and hot sauce and does it all with love. It's a tasty way to start the day and is also good for lunch or dinner. (As you know, Rach loves "BLD"—breakfast, lunch or dinner—recipes!) If you're in New Jersey, stop in for a sandwich at Johnny's Pork Roll & Coffee Too in Red Bank, or look for his food truck—emblazoned with the logo "Eat More Pork Roll!"—in Asbury Park.

For more recipes from our "BLD" show, check out Rach’s Flatbread Pizzas, 3 Ways With Eggs and Katie Lee Biegel's Women's Club Baked Chicken Legs and Kale Slaw.
