WWE RAW Spoiler Lineup For 10/16 Season Premiere

WWE has a big season premiere planned for Oklahoma City this week. They are also going to set the stage for Saudi Arabia’s Crown Jewel event, while Survivor Series season is still in the air. Now, we know what to expect this week.

WWE’s ‘season premiere’ of Monday Night RAW airs tonight on the USA Network and brings with it two title matches. The card for the evening is already set to include the Undisputed WWE Tag Team Championship bout, where Cody Rhodes and Jey Uso defend their title against Damian Priest and Finn Balor. Additionally, GUNTHER puts his Intercontinental Championship on the line against Bronson Reed. The night will also feature a Falls Count Anywhere match between Ricochet and Shinsuke Nakamura, Rhea Ripley taking on Shayna Baszler, and a clash between Piper Niven and Natalya.

PW Insider noted the spoiler lineup for RAW this week. It appears that they have a loaded show in store for us this week.

-Sami Zayn promo.

-Ricochet vs. Shinsuke Nakamura: Falls Count Anywhere.

-Natalya vs. Piper Niven.

-Seth Rollins promo.

-Johnny Gargano vs. Ludvg Kaiser.

-Rhea Ripley vs. Shayna Baszler.  Ripley is also slated to get promo time tonight.

-WWE Intercontinental Champion GUNTHER vs. Bronson Reed.

-Cody Rhodes & Jey Uso vs. Judgment Day.  This is slated to run multiple segments and get a lot of time, although that is dependent on how the earlier segments run live.

It was also noted that, “Indus Sher are also slated to appear at some point tonight in their first TV appearance in some time.”

We will have to see how it all pans out when the show goes live. As always, we will have comprehensive coverage, right here at Ringside News.
