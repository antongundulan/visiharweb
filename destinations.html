Hotel Nordoy Review, Tel Aviv, Israel

Tel Aviv, Israel

With its dusky pink façade and distinctive, dome-topped roof, this place is a local landmark. Built in 1925, it’s been artfully updated, mixing period features with cool contemporary design. Charming service and a resident cat add to the appeal – as does the rooftop terrace with sunset views and help-yourself wine.
