Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jackie Martling was born on February 14, 1948, in Mineola, Long Island, New York. He graduated from Michigan State University with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. Martling’s career took a significant turn when he joined The Howard Stern Show in 1983. He served as the head writer for the show for over 15 years, contributing to its immense popularity. His witty jokes and humorous personality made him a favorite among the audience.
