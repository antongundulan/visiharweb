Spoiler on Closing Match For WWE Survivor Series Event

WWE’s final premium live event of the year is set to go down in Chicago’s Allstate Arena. As rumors and hopes of CM Punk’s return to come company linger, we do know for sure that WWE is preparing to present a big five-match card for Survivor Series.

Ringside News has complete coverage of the entire Survivor Series event. There are ton of headlines for the show, but we have compiled them all in one place for you.

WWE Survivor Series is happening on Saturday, November 25th, starting at 8 PM ET on Peacock. The pre-show begins at 7 PM ET on WWE’s social channels and Peacock. As of this writing, there are no kickoff show matches for Survivor Series.

PW Insider reports that the main event for tonight’s WWE Survivor Series will be the 5-on-5 Men’s WarGames match. That match sees Drew McIntyre and the Judgement Day vs. Seth Rollins, Cody Rhodes, Sami Zayn, Jey Uso & Randy Orton. This should be expected, but now we have confirmation.

WWE likes to sandwich WarGames matches, so it stands to reason to assume that the event will start with the women’s WarGames match. That was not a part of this report, but it is expected at this point.

We will have to see what big angles WWE has in store for WarGames, and the entire Survivor Series event. Triple H likes to present loaded, to the point, shows, and Survivor Series certainly has a lot of possibilities.

WWE 2023 Survivor Series Card:

  • GUNTHER vs The Miz – Intercontinental Title
  • Rhea Ripley vs Zoey Stark – WWE Women’s World Title
  • Judgement Day and Drew McIntyre vs Seth Rollins, Sami Zayn, Jey Uso, Cody Rhodes and Randy Orton – WarGames Match
  • Damage CTRL vs Bianca Belair, Charlotte Flair, Shotzi & Becky Lynch
  • Dragon Lee vs Santos Escobar
