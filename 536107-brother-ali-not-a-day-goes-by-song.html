Not A Day Goes By

Check out Not A Day Goes By, the latest track from Brother Ali, which dropped on Wednesday, May 2nd, 2012. Brother Ali's movement grows stronger with each new release, and Not A Day Goes By has further strengthened that fact. It's a solid addition to the commendable body of work Brother Ali has been creating since stepping foot in the rap game, and we're definitely looking forward to what Brother Ali has on deck.
