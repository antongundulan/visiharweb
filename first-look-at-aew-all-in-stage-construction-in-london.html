First Look At AEW All In Stage Construction In London

AEW has seen a ton of growth over the course of four years and it continues to make history with their upcoming events. AEW All In is certainly the company’s biggest event yet and now it appears fans have finally gotten a first look at the All In stage construction in London.

AEW will be holding the next All In event at Wembley Stadium this year, which will have capacity of 90,000. Obviously, everyone who doubted the event had to eat their words as the event ended up selling a lot of tickets. The event has already beaten WrestleMania 3 in ticket sales, SummerSlam 1992 and finally WrestleMania 32.

As revealed by various photos making their way online, the construction for AEW All In’s stage at Wembley Stadium is going quite well. Naturally, fans are very excited for the historic event.

With so many matches already being announced for All In, we will have to wait and see whether the event will end up becoming one of the most successful events in the history of pro wrestling. After all, AEW has a ton of momentum heading into the August 27th event.
