Baskin-Robbins debuts two new summer treats and customers will love the sweet deals

ICE cream eaters now have two more options to choose from at Baskin-Robbins as the flavor maker has launched new menu items.

Baskin-Robbins is offering two new flavors for the month of July to celebrate National Ice Cream Month.

Starting July 1, customers can score the OREO MEGA STUF Cone and the Cappuccino Blast.

The OREO MEGA STUF Cone is an OREO-flavored waffle cone with OREO creme drizzled inside, two scoops of its classic Cookies ‘n Cream Ice Cream, and another layer of OREO creme filling.

The treat also comes topped with whipped cream and OREO cookie pieces to finish. 

The ice cream brand is also launching the new Cappuccino Blast also with drizzles of OREO creme.

"When Baskin-Robbins celebrates National Ice Cream Month, we go big," Jeanne Bolger, director of product research and development for Baskin-Robbins said in a press release.

“This July, guests can experience the best part of an OREO – the creme," she added.

Both will be available at participating Baskin-Robbins until sold out.

SUNDAY FUNDAE

In addition to the two new offerings, Baskin-Robbins also announced a new Flavor of the Month called Sundae Funday.

The flavor comes with a combo of vanilla and chocolate ice creams mixed with fudge swirls, crunchy chocolate-dipped waffle cone pieces, rainbow sprinkles, and a cherry.

The company is also giving out some "extra sweet deals" to celebrate the Month.

Starting on National Ice Cream Day, July 16, through July 22, customers can score $5 off their purchase of $20 or more through DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Postmates.

Guests just use the promo code SEIZETHEYAY on all BaskinRobbins.com or Baskin-Robbins app orders.

Those who shop in-store can redeem the in-app coupon at checkout.

To end the celebration, on July 31, guests can enjoy 31 percent off all scoops at shops nationwide.

MORE ICE CREAM NEWS

Baskin-Robbins rival Dairy Queen also has a new offering for customers to try the cake batter cookie dough blizzard.

The flavor was launched on June 26 at select U.S. stores and costs about $4.46 and $6.50, depending on location.

The new flavor features confetti cookie dough pieces, and sprinkles, as well as a mix of both cake batter and Dairy Queen's traditional soft-serve ice cream.

The cake batter flavor will be a part of Dairy Queen's July Blizzard of the Month offering – but only be available for a limited time.
