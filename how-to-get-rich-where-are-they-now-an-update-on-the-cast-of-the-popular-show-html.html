How to Get Rich: Where Are They Now? An Update on the Cast of the Popular Show

Spread the love             

The reality TV sensation “How to Get Rich” took Netflix by storm, featuring financial guru Ramit Sethi guiding people through money matters. We followed their journeys as they faced financial challenges and sought a path to prosperity. Now, let’s delve into where the cast members are today, after the cameras stopped rolling.

Nathalie Dahan: From Financial Struggles to Entrepreneurial Triumph

Nathalie, once facing financial hardships, showcased her West Hollywood restaurant’s struggles on the show. Today, she’s the CEO of Maison Jolie Designs, serving high-end clients. Nathalie, now happily married to model Simo Fanny, resides in Beverly Hills, California, aiming for a promising career in the entertainment sector.

Sophina DeJesus: Overcoming Budget Woes to Pursue Passions

Facing housing challenges, Sophina sold her condo as advised by Ramit, saving money for her passions and travels. Currently renting in Los Angeles, she maintains a close bond with her mother and teaches gymnastics to girls in Berkeley. Sophina is thriving, and her private classes are sought after at $150 per hour.

Drew Reebof Refky: Transforming Finances and Finding Happiness

Struggling financially, Drew relied on irregular drag show earnings. After financial honesty with his fiancée Mikey, they worked together to overcome challenges. Drew secured a better job, paid off credit card debt, and now happily resides in Chicago, Illinois, with his spouse and their dog, Gigi. Drag remains a vital part of his life.

Millie Oquendo and Christian Cardenas: Navigating Financial Hurdles in Marriage

Engaged couple Millie and Christian faced financial hurdles, with stock losses and an expensive auto loan. Resolving their issues, they now enjoy a happy marriage in Elizabeth, New Jersey. Millie pursued additional income sources, while Christian remains committed to his salaried job, even earning a real estate license for New Jersey.

Frank Grimsley: From Debt to Social Media Stardom

Known as the season four champion of “The Circle,” Frank struggled with saving money and splurging on non-essentials. Ramit’s guidance led to wise investments and a debt repayment plan. Frank left his role as a school social worker to focus on social media influencing, finding success and residing in Maryland.

Sara Ehlers and Reginald “Reggie” Guinto: Wedding Dreams Fulfilled

Newly engaged Sara and Reggie sought help in managing wedding expenses and finances. Compromising, they celebrated a beautiful Italian ceremony and now reside in Los Angeles. Sara works as the Associate Director of Influencer Marketing at Village Marketing, while Reggie serves as an Engineering Manager with Boeing.

Conclusion:

The cast of “How to Get Rich” has undergone remarkable transformations, overcoming financial struggles and building brighter futures. From entrepreneurial ventures to social media stardom, each cast member has found their path to financial success. Their stories serve as inspiration, reminding us that with determination and guidance, anyone can achieve financial well-being.
