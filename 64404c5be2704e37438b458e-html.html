Messi fails in his attempt at camouflage and drives fans wild at Disneyland Paris

Lionel Messi took advantage of a day off to go with his three children and his wife Antonela Roccuzzo to a ride at the Disneyland Paris amusement park and failed in his attempt to camouflage to go unnoticed.

He was wearing a hood and at times a mask and was detected, which caused a craze, because it is not always so handy to be the best soccer player in the world.

People wanted to take pictures and ask for Messi's autograph, who had a rest day granted by coach Christophe Galtier to the team, after Saturday's 3-1 PSG victory over Lens at the Parc des Princes, with a goal by La Pulga.

The Rosarino wore a black, hooded sweatshirt, but it didn't take long for him to be recognized, also because Antonela is famous in her own right and is also easy to spot along with her children.

Fans shared photos with Messi at Euro Disney

The photos of the fans began to circulate on social media and Messi could be seen with his son Mateo holding his hand, while Ciro and Thiago were with their mother.

They were accompanied by Cesc Fabregas' wife Daniella Semaan and their children, who also appeared in the photos of the fans and Antonela posted a story with a picture of her, in which they both wear the same white jackets with Mickey Mouse sleeves, already tired of walking, and it can be seen on Rocuzzo's cell phone that it was almost 11pm.

Lionel Messi's next match with PSG is this Friday when they visit Angers and look to extend the eight-point lead they have over Olympique de Marseille to win Ligue 1 with seven games left to play.
