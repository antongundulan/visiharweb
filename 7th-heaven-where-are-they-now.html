7Th Heaven Where Are They Now?

The family drama series “7th Heaven,” which aired from 1996 to 2007, was a staple of American television, capturing the hearts of viewers with its portrayal of the Camden family. The show, created by Brenda Hampton, followed the lives of Reverend Eric Camden, his wife Annie, and their seven children. As the series concluded, fans were left wondering what became of the actors who played their beloved characters. In this article, we’ll explore where the cast of “7th Heaven” is now, delving into their careers and personal lives post-show.

Stephen Collins as Eric Camden

Stephen Collins played the family patriarch, Reverend Eric Camden. After “7th Heaven,” Collins continued acting, appearing in shows like “No Ordinary Family” and “Revolution.” However, his career was overshadowed by controversy in 2014 when he admitted to past incidents of sexual misconduct with minors. This led to a significant downturn in his career, and he has since kept a low profile.

Catherine Hicks as Annie Camden

Catherine Hicks portrayed Annie Camden, the nurturing mother of the Camden clan. Post-“7th Heaven,” Hicks appeared in several movies and TV shows, including “My Name Is Jerry” and guest roles on series like “The Young and the Restless.” She has also been active in theater productions and continues to act in both film and television.

Barry Watson as Matt Camden

Barry Watson, who played the eldest Camden child, Matt, went on to star in shows like “What About Brian” and “Samantha Who?” He also had a recurring role on “Gossip Girl” and starred in the UPtv series “Date My Dad.” Watson has balanced his acting career with his role as a father to his three children.

Jessica Biel as Mary Camden

Jessica Biel’s role as Mary Camden launched her into stardom. After leaving the show, Biel’s career soared with leading roles in films like “The Illusionist” and “Total Recall.” She also received critical acclaim for her performance in the limited series “The Sinner,” which she also executive produced. Biel is married to singer Justin Timberlake, and they have two children together.

Beverley Mitchell as Lucy Camden-Kinkirk

Beverley Mitchell, who played Lucy Camden-Kinkirk, continued her acting career with a role in the TV movie “I Know What I Saw” and starred in the Pop series “Hollywood Darlings.” She also pursued a music career, releasing a country album in 2007. Mitchell is married with children and often shares her life as a mother on social media.

David Gallagher as Simon Camden

David Gallagher, known for his role as Simon Camden, took a break from acting to attend college after “7th Heaven.” He returned to television with guest appearances on shows like “CSI: Miami” and “Criminal Minds.” Gallagher has also lent his voice to video game characters and continues to act in various projects.

Mackenzie Rosman as Ruthie Camden

Mackenzie Rosman portrayed the youngest daughter, Ruthie Camden. Post-show, Rosman appeared in the horror film “Beneath” and had a recurring role on “The Secret Life of the American Teenager.” She is also an accomplished equestrian and is active in raising awareness for cystic fibrosis.

Lorenzo Brino as Sam and David Camden

Lorenzo Brino, one of the quadruplets who played the Camden twins Sam and David, sadly passed away in a car accident in March 2020 at the age of 21. His untimely death was mourned by his family, friends, and “7th Heaven” fans.

Nikolas Brino as Sam and David Camden

Nikolas Brino, another of the quadruplets, has stayed out of the spotlight since “7th Heaven.” Unlike his brother Lorenzo, Nikolas has not pursued an acting career and has kept a low profile regarding his personal life and career endeavors.

George Stults as Kevin Kinkirk

George Stults, who played Lucy’s husband Kevin Kinkirk, has appeared in various TV movies and had guest roles on shows like “Hydra” and “Zoe Gone.” He continues to act and remains close with his former “7th Heaven” co-stars, particularly his real-life brother Geoff Stults.

Ashlee Simpson as Cecilia Smith

Ashlee Simpson, who had a recurring role as Cecilia Smith, pursued a music career with hits like “Pieces of Me.” She starred in the reality TV series “The Ashlee Simpson Show” and later “Ashlee + Evan” with her husband Evan Ross. Simpson has released multiple albums and continues to work in the entertainment industry.

Adam LaVorgna as Robbie Palmer

Adam LaVorgna, who played Robbie Palmer, took a hiatus from acting to focus on his education. He returned to the screen with roles in independent films and guest spots on television series. LaVorgna remains active in the acting community and engages with fans through social media.

Jeremy London as Chandler Hampton

Jeremy London, known for his role as Chandler Hampton, continued acting with appearances in films and television shows. He has faced personal challenges, including substance abuse, but has worked towards recovery. London continues to act and has taken on roles in independent films.

Conclusion

The cast of “7th Heaven” has experienced a mix of continued success, personal challenges, and tragedy since the show ended. While some have remained in the public eye, others have chosen more private lives. Their journeys post-“7th Heaven” reflect a diverse array of paths, much like the characters they portrayed on the beloved family drama.
