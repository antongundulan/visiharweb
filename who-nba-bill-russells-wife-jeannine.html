Who is Bill Russell's wife?

NBA icon, Bill Russell, was married numerous times throughout his lifetime.

On July 31, 2022, the 88-year-old passed away with his wife, Jeannine, by his side.

Who is Bill Russell's wife?

At the time of his death, Bill Russell was married to Jeannine.

Jeannine Russell was born on January 31, 1967. She is also a former professional golfer.

In 2010, Jeannine was ranked 15th place during the CN Canadian Women's Tour. In the same year, she won Gold Canada's Player of the Year.

Jeannine and Bill often played golf together according to social media.

Jeannine is Bill’s fourth marriage. He was previously married to Rose Swisher (1956–1973), Dorothy Anstett (1977–1980), and Marilyn Nault (1996–2009).

Who are Bill Russell's children?

Bill Russell is survived by three children: Jacob, William Russell Jr, and Karen.

He shares all three of his children with his first wife and college sweetheart, Rose Swisher, whom he divorced in 1973.

Bill Russell’s daughter, Karen, was born in 1962.

She is a legal and political analyst who graduated from Georgetown University and Harvard Law School.

Karen has served as the Development Director for the Robert F Kennedy Memorial Center for Human Rights and has also contributed to publications like The New York Times.

On July 31, 2022, she tweeted a series of pictures with her late father, captioned: "Keanu Reeves on death, 'I know that the ones who love us will miss us.'"

William Jr was born in 1957 but passed away from cancer in 2016.

Jacob was born in 1959 and is reportedly happily married.

Bill and Jeannine do not share any biological children together.

What was Bill Russell's net worth?

At his time of death, Bill's net worth was estimated to be $10million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

The 88-year-old was a former professional basketball player for the NBA's Boston Celtics from 1956 to 1969.

Over the course of his 13-year career, Russell won a total of 11 NBA championships.
