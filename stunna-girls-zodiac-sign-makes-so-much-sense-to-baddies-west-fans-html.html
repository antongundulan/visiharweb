Stunna Girl’s Real Name:

Stunna Girl’s stage name might be what we know her by, but her real name is Suzanne Sade Brown. From Spotify to Instagram and, of course, Baddies West, she’s embraced the name Stunna Girl with pride. In her own words, “Well, I was named after the singer Sade. I always looked up to her. I thought she was so cool.” So, Suzanne Sade Brown became Stunna Girl, and the rest is history.

Stunna Girl’s Hometown:

Stunna Girl proudly calls Sacramento, California, her hometown. In a 2018 interview with DJ Smallz Eyes, she pinpointed Oak Park as her exact neighborhood. Growing up in Sacramento wasn’t always a walk in the park for Stunna Girl. She recalled, “Growing up in Sacramento, there’s a lot of haters and jealousy,” shedding light on the challenges she faced.

The toughest part of her upbringing was when her mother was incarcerated for two years. At just 12 years old, Stunna Girl found herself in a different life situation. These experiences undoubtedly shaped her into the strong and determined artist we know today.
