Does vitamin C lighten skin?

Lightens dark spots

Vitamin C helps lighten dark spots on the skin by restricting the enzyme responsible for the production of melanin. Dark spots form due to an overproduction of melanin, a dark pigment, in your skin.

Can vitamin C whiten skin?

The science of vitamin C reveals it effectively lightens skin, mainly by decreasing the amount of melanin pigment skin cells make. The research shows topical treatment with vitamin C or derivatives, combined with dietary sources, is more effective than oral supplements.

How long does vitamin C take to lighten skin?
