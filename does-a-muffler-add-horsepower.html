Does muffler reduce horsepower?

Mufflers and resonators make exhaust quieter by placing restrictions in its path. These restrictions force the engine's exhaust to flow around them, reducing noise emission and robbing horsepower in the process.

Does a muffler make your car faster?

An engine is able to generate more power, faster if it is able to get rid of all of the exhaust gases it produces quickly. By their nature, mufflers restrict exhaust flow or create back pressure, which slightly slows down your engine.

Can you gain horsepower from exhaust?

To put it simply – yes. An aftermarket exhaust can provide additional horsepower to a car. They work by providing a better airflow for exhaust gases to escape the combustion chamber – providing more room for fresh air to enter and create a bigger combustion on the next cycle.

Does a straight pipe add horsepower?

The primary advantage of equipping a straight pipe exhaust to a performance engine is that you'll see a definite boost in your horsepower. This result occurs because the system reduces the amount of backpressure from the engine, allowing the exhaust gases to form with greater freedom.
