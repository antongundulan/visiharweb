Latest Ticket Sales Update for AEW Worlds End 2023

The AEW Worlds End 2023 pay-per-view event is scheduled to occur on Saturday, December 30, 2023, at the Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum in Long Island, New York. The action kicks off with the pre-show at 7:00 PM Eastern Time, followed by the main card at 8:00 PM Eastern Time.

As of the latest update from WrestleTix, the event has sold 7,726 tickets, with 1,587 tickets still available for purchase. The venue has a total capacity set up for 9,313 attendees.

Here’s the updated lineup for AEW Worlds End:

AEW World Championship Match: MJF (c) vs. Samoa Joe

AEW Women’s Championship Match: ‘Timeless’ Toni Storm (c) vs. Riho

AEW Tag Team Championship Match: Ricky Starks & Big Bill (c) vs. Golden Jets (Kenny Omega and Chris Jericho)

AEW Continental Classic Tournament Final: To be determined vs. To be determined

With these exciting matchups on the card, AEW Worlds End promises to be a thrilling event for wrestling fans to close out the year 2023.

As a reminder, Ringside News will have live play-by-play coverage of the Worlds Event over in our AEW Results hub.
