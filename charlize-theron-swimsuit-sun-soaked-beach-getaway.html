Charlize Theron soaks up the sun in a swimsuit on beach getaway

Spring may be taking its sweet time officially landing Stateside, but Charlize Theron's ready to embrace the heat we've got.

The actress took to Instagram to share photographs from her beach getaway, and it looked like she was fully feeling the late spring vibe.

She included pictures of herself downing a chilled glass of champagne while lounging on a beach chair, surrounded by sand and palm trees.

Charlize wore a white swimsuit with a pair of sunglasses and a straw hat under which she'd tucked her blonde tresses, captioning her shots: "A small appreciation toast to spring. Don’t be shy. We ready for you."

Fans quickly inundated her comments section with heart emojis as one also commented: "Big slay queen," and another added: "You are so gorgeous," while a third gushed: "Enjoyyyy your vacation."

The actress has been spending time recently focussing more on her charitable efforts with Charlize Theron Africa Outreach Program and being at home with her daughters August, seven, and Jackson, 11.

She last made a high profile appearance at Dior's Paris Fashion Week show in the last week of February, joining the likes of Gal Gadot and BLACKPINK's Jisoo.

The Monster actress opted for a minimal yet fashion forward ensemble from the house, a silver-white macrame dress with delicate sequins, featuring a sheer skirt and fringe reaching past the hem.
