How Much Does a Pump It Up Birthday Party Cost?

Pump It Up is a franchise of indoor entertainment centers that specialize in hosting children’s birthday parties. Pump It Up birthday parties are known for their inflatable playgrounds, which include bounce houses, slides, obstacle courses, and more. If you’re a parent considering hosting a Pump It Up birthday party for your child, one of your questions may be: how much does a Pump It Up birthday party cost?

The cost of a Pump It Up birthday party will depend on several factors, including the location of the Pump It Up center you choose, the type of party package you select, and the number of guests you invite. However, as a general rule, you can expect to spend anywhere from $250 to $500 for a basic Pump It Up birthday party package. This price usually includes 90 minutes of playtime in the inflatables area, followed by 30 minutes in a private party room for cake, food, and gift opening.

Of course, the price will vary depending on the location of the Pump It Up center that you select. For example, a party in Los Angeles or New York City is likely to cost more than a party in a smaller city or town. You can inquire directly with your local Pump It Up center to find out their specific pricing for birthday parties.

Frequently Asked Questions about Pump It Up Birthday Parties

What does a Pump It Up birthday party package include?

A standard Pump It Up birthday party package usually includes 90 minutes of playtime in the inflatable playground, followed by 30 minutes in a private party room for cake, food, and gift opening. Most packages also include food and drinks for the guests, including pizza, drinks, and/or snacks. The number of guests included in the package will depend on which package you choose.

Can I bring outside food to a Pump It Up birthday party?

Most Pump It Up centers have strict rules about outside food and drink being brought into the facility. In most cases, you will be required to purchase any food or drinks you would like to serve your guests from the Pump It Up facility. However, some centers may allow certain exceptions, such as bringing in a birthday cake or other desserts. It’s best to check with your selected Pump It Up location for their specific rules regarding outside food.

What kind of inflatables can we expect at a Pump It Up birthday party?

Pump It Up centers typically have a variety of inflatable playground equipment, including bounce houses, obstacle courses, slides, and more. The specific equipment available will vary depending on the location of the facility. You can inquire with your local Pump It Up center to find out what specific inflatables they have available at their location.

Can we bring our own decorations to a Pump It Up birthday party?

Most Pump It Up centers have strict rules about decorations being brought into the facility. In most cases, you will be allowed to bring in small decorations like balloons or table centerpieces, but larger decorations like banners or wall hangings will not be allowed. It’s best to check with your selected Pump It Up location for their specific rules regarding decorations.

Are Pump It Up birthday parties suitable for all ages?

Pump It Up centers are designed for children between the ages of 2 and 12. However, some locations may have special party packages or party rooms that can accommodate older children or adults. It’s best to check with your selected Pump It Up location to find out if they have any special packages for older guests.

What happens if a child gets injured while playing in the inflatables?

Pump It Up centers take safety very seriously and have strict rules in place to prevent injuries from occurring. However, accidents can happen. In the event that a child is injured while playing in the inflatables, a trained staff member will respond immediately to provide first aid and assess the situation. In serious cases, emergency services may be called.

How far in advance should I book a Pump It Up birthday party?

It’s recommended that you book your Pump It Up birthday party at least 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure that you are able to secure the desired date and time for your event. However, some locations may require even more advance notice for booking. It’s best to check with your selected Pump It Up location for their specific booking policies.

What happens if I need to cancel or reschedule my Pump It Up birthday party?

Most Pump It Up centers have specific policies in place for cancellations and rescheduling. In most cases, you will need to provide notice of cancellation or rescheduling at least a certain number of days in advance to avoid incurring any fees or penalties. It’s best to check with your selected Pump It Up location for their specific cancellation and rescheduling policies.

Are there any rules or guidelines for guests attending a Pump It Up birthday party?

Yes, Pump It Up centers have strict rules and guidelines in place to ensure the safety and enjoyment of all guests. Some common rules include wearing socks in the inflatables area, refraining from roughhousing or aggressive behavior, and following the instructions of the staff members. It’s important to review these rules with your guests prior to attending the party to ensure everyone is aware of the guidelines.

Can I request special accommodations for guests with disabilities?

Yes, Pump It Up centers strive to accommodate guests with disabilities or special needs to the best of their ability. It’s recommended that you contact your selected Pump It Up location in advance to discuss any special accommodations you may need.

What kind of add-ons or extras are available for Pump It Up birthday parties?

Many Pump It Up centers offer additional party add-ons or extras, such as party favors, balloons, or special activities. The specific add-ons available will vary depending on the location of the facility. It’s best to inquire with your selected Pump It Up location for their specific add-ons and extras.

How many staff members will be present during my Pump It Up birthday party?

Pump It Up centers typically have a staff member present in each inflatable area to supervise and ensure the safety of the children. Additionally, there will typically be a staff member assigned to your party to help with setup, serving food, and cleanup. The number of staff members present will depend on the size of your party and the specific center you select.

Can I bring my own music to play during the party?

Most Pump It Up centers have their own sound system and music that will be played during the party. However, you may be able to request a specific song or playlist to be played during your event. It’s best to inquire with your selected Pump It Up location for their specific policies regarding music.
