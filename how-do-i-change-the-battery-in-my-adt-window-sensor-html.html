How do I change the battery in my ADT window sensor?

How to change your battery:

Put the alarm system in test mode. Take the cover off of your sensor. Pry out the old battery. Insert the new battery in the proper place. Your battery is now replaced. Don’t forget to take the alarm out of test mode!

How do I stop my ADT alarm from beeping with low battery?

For many of our most popular systems, beeping alarms and low battery trouble beeps can be silenced by pressing the [OFF] or [#] buttons from the keypad. If this does not work, please refer to your system user’s manual for specific instructions.
