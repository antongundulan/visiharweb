Nearly 34% Of People Think Is The Weirdest Celebrity Couple

When Elon Musk and Grimes showed up together at the 2018 Met Gala, per Cosmopolitan, it would be forgivable if you had assumed that their date was merely a PR stunt meant to stir up attention. However, the two were apparently a genuine couple and have even gone on to have a child together. But that doesn't mean that they're a normal pair by any means. In fact, 33.65% of people who took Nicki Swift's survey feel like they are the weirdest celebrity couple around. Cosmo even claimed that "[t]hey're basically the Romeo and Juliet of our time." Bold assertion? *Nods head vigorously.* Honestly, though, while the comparison to classic Shakespearian star-crossed lovers is definitely debatable, there's certainly no denying that they're an unexpected, if intriguing, pair.

Although some people might be upset that their relationship is considered to be weird, Musk and Grimes would likely be thrilled by the distinction. That's because they have both found success by being different. Musk is a man set on living on Mars while Grimes is a singer who creates alien-worthy music with a supervillain-like aesthetic. And as strange as you might think Grimes' art really is, she admitted to The Guardian, "In my life, I'm a lot more weird than this." Of course, that can be proven by the fact that Musk and Grimes named their son X Æ A-Xii. 

Yeah, they're not a typical pair and that seems to be just the way that they like it.
