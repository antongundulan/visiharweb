Is there a weapon in Dying Light 2 that doesn't break?

Every Dying Light 2 weapon will continue to lose durability and eventually break the more you use it. While some weapons are stronger than others (and the aforementioned modification method can “repair” them), there is currently no official way to make a weapon unbreakable.

Does Dying Light 2 have weapon durability?

There is a way to have basically unlimited weapon durability in Dying Light 2 by way of the Korek Charm. This item is hidden in a secret developer room that anyone can unlock with a little patience after progressing to a certain point in the game.
